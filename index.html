About

Curtis is a 23 year old Ohio based composer, musician, game developer and artist. He has been creating music and videogames for over 13 years. Curtis graduated from the University of Akron in 2022 with two bachelor's degrees in music composition and woodwind performance. While primarily trained in classical clarinet performance, he additionally plays keyboard, guitar and bass. When he's not writing music or code, Curtis enjoys drawing, watching movies, and relaxing with his cat Rousseau.
